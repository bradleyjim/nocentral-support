NoCentral Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

Data Collection

NoCentral does not collect, transmit, or store any user data outside of your device.

Local Storage

  • Site configurations (VC IP addresses, usernames) are stored locally on your device
  • Credentials are stored securely in the system Keychain
  • All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any third-party servers

Network Communication

  • NoCentral communicates only with your local Aruba Virtual Controller
  • All communication occurs over your local network
  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.
