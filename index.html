Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

iOS 16+ and macOS Taho 26.3.1

Does this work with HPE InstantOn?

No, NoCentral only works with Aruba IAP clusters running AOS 8.10-8.13.

Do I need an Aruba Central subscription?

No, NoCentral works directly with your Virtual Controller over your local network.

How do I connect to my Virtual Controller?

Enter your VC's IP address, username, and password. NoCentral will securely store credentials in your system Keychain.
